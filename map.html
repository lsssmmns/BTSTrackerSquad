BTS Tour History

This following map visualizes every BTS tour from 2014 to 2020. Concerts are depicted by points on the map which vary in size depending on the size of the venue they were held at.

To use the map :
Navigate the map using the interactive timeline bar at the bottom of the map by clicking on a year of interest or dragging slider through the years.
Hover over points on map to learn more about the concert venue and tour.
