Spotify Chart History

The following map visualizes the history of BTS's charted songs on Spotify by year and by country. BTS songs that had charted on a country's Spotify Hot 200 charts are highlighted in purple, with the shade of purple representing the number of songs charted in the given country in that year.

To use the map :
Click on the year of interest to view the songs charted by BTS in the requested year.
To learn more about the charted songs in a country, click on the country of interest and check out the pop-up in the top right corner.
